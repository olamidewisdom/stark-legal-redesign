What We Do

Practice Areas

From complex energy transactions to high-stakes dispute resolution, our practice spans the full spectrum of commercial law in Nigeria and internationally.

01

Energy

Advising on oil & gas transactions, upstream and downstream operations, power sector regulation, and renewable energy projects across Nigeria's dynamic energy landscape.

02

Transport & Aviation

Comprehensive legal services across aviation, maritime, and road transport — from regulatory compliance and aircraft financing to cargo disputes and liability.

03

Financial Services

Deep expertise in banking regulation, capital markets, insurance law, fintech compliance, and structured finance. We guide financial institutions through Nigeria's evolving regulatory landscape with precision and commercial foresight — advising on landmark transactions and staying at the forefront of the sector.

04

Corporate & Commercial

M&A, joint ventures, corporate restructuring, contract drafting, and commercial advisory for businesses operating in and across Nigeria's evolving market.

05

Dispute Resolution

Litigation, arbitration, and mediation services. We represent clients in Nigerian courts and before international arbitral tribunals with strategic, results-driven advocacy.
